Ladino: La Lingua Biva




"La lingua biva" means "the living language," and is an expsession used to describe the language of the Sephardic Jews, called Ladino or Djudeo-Espanyol. This website aims to work towards spreading knowledge and awareness of the language, keeping it alive by exposing new people to the language. Use the menu bar at the top of the page to navigate to pages about Ladino, important people in the Ladino community, and other resources that you can find online about Ladino.
