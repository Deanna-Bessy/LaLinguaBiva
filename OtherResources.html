Other Resources

  • www.myladino.com myladino.com has tons of pages full of examples of Ladino, bios about people who speak Ladino and spread its culture to others, and much more.
  • www.sephardicstudies.org A website by the Foundation For The Advancement of Sephardic Studies and Culture. It contains a section called Ladinokomunidad, which is a forum where people can post all kinds of content in the Ladino language.
  • https://www.youtube.com/user/AutoridadLadino/videos A Youtube channel by Autoridad Nasionala del Ladino i su Kultura that interviews, speeches, and some sing-alongs in Ladino.
  • https://www.youtube.com/user/repaeu/videos A Youtube channel that has three intro-lesson videos for Ladino.
